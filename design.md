# JobPilot AI - Design System

## Design Philosophy

JobPilot AI should feel:

- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Professional
- Productive
- Human

Avoid:

- Neon colors
- Excessive gradients
- Pure white backgrounds
- Heavy glassmorphism
- Crypto startup aesthetics

Users may spend hours reviewing resumes and jobs.

The interface should reduce visual fatigue.
